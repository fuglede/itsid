Democratizing Deterministic AI

We started Itsid with a simple question: what if an AI model could achieve perfect fidelity? Not 99.9%. Not "close enough." Exactly 100%, every time, for every input.

Our Mission

Making Identity Transforms
Accessible to Everyone

The AI industry has been obsessed with making models that transform inputs into different outputs. We asked: what about the developers who need their output to be exactly the same as their input - but generated by AI?

Whether you're navigating complex licensing requirements, need cryptographic proof that your content was AI-generated, or simply value the peace of mind that comes with deterministic output, Itsid was built for you.

Our Values

What We Stand For

Determinism First

In a world of probabilistic AI, we champion certainty. Our model produces the same output for the same input, every single time. No temperature settings. No randomness. Just identity.

Radical Transparency

We publish our benchmarks, our architecture, and our fidelity metrics. When your output is always identical to your input, you have nothing to hide.

Developer-Obsessed

Every feature we build starts with a developer need. From our standard-compatible API to our CI/CD pipeline integrations, we make identity transforms fit naturally into your workflow.

Our Journey

Key Milestones

July 2023
The "Identity Paper"
Co-founders publish "Towards Identity-Preserving Language Models: A 14-Billion Parameter Approach" at IdentityML workshop. It receives mixed reviews but passionate supporters.
January 2024
Itsid ApS Founded
Company incorporated in Copenhagen, Denmark. Seed round of DKK 29M led by Recursive Ventures, with participation from Identity Capital and Launchpad Fellows (LF24 batch).
June 2024
itsid-v1.0 Launch
First public release of the API. 7B parameter model achieves 100% fidelity on all benchmarks. Waitlist hits 12,000 developers in the first week.
November 2024
Series A - DKK 193M
Series A led by Sequoia Meridian, valuing the company at DKK 1.24B. Lead partner calls it "the most honest AI company I've ever seen."
March 2025
itsid-v3.0 - 14B Parameters
Launch of the 14-billion parameter model with unlimited context length. Enterprise tier introduced with custom model training and dedicated Identity Engineers.
March 2026
50,000+ Developers
Itsid surpasses 50,000 active developers. itsid-v3.1 released with enhanced streaming performance and Advanced Identity Mode™. The future is deterministic.
