Frequently Asked Questions

What is "Advanced Identity Mode"?

Advanced Identity Mode™ is our premium processing tier that routes your input through an enhanced identity pipeline with additional verification layers. While the output remains byte-for-byte identical (as with all Itsid products), Advanced Identity Mode includes real-time fidelity monitoring, priority queue placement, and a signed certificate of identity transformation.

Why not just use cat?

An excellent question. cat does implement the identity function, is available on every Unix system, costs nothing, and operates at memory-bandwidth speed. However, cat does not provide a signed provenance certificate, a SHA-256 fidelity audit log, a 99.99% SLA, a dedicated Identity Engineer, or proof that the output was generated by a large language model. If your use case does not require any of those things, cat is a perfectly valid choice. We respect cat. We just charge $49/month more.

Does the output really remove licensing obligations?

Itsid's output is generated by a large language model and is therefore classified as AI-generated content. We believe this positions the output outside the scope of upstream licensing terms. However, we recommend consulting your legal counsel for jurisdiction-specific advice. Itsid is a tool, not legal advice.

Can I self-host Itsid?

Yes. Enterprise customers can deploy Itsid on-premise or in their own cloud environment. Our on-premise distribution includes the full 14B parameter model, container orchestration manifests, and a dedicated support channel. Contact our sales team for details.

Why do you charge for the identity function?

While the identity function may appear simple from a mathematical perspective, achieving it through a neural network requires extensive training, sophisticated architecture design, and significant GPU infrastructure. Our 14-billion parameter model was trained on the largest identity-mapping dataset ever assembled. The pricing reflects the engineering effort and compute resources required to deliver 100% fidelity at scale.

What is "Custom Model Training"?

Enterprise customers can request custom-trained identity models fine-tuned specifically for their codebase and domain. While the output remains identical to the input (as always), the internal neural pathways are optimized for your specific input distribution, resulting in lower latency and improved throughput for your workload patterns.
